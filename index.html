About us

Welcome to Nclex Nepal Pvt Ltd, where your journey to international healthcare excellence begins. As a premier Nclex Review Center, we take pride in not only preparing nurses for success in their Nclex examinations but also in guiding them towards lucrative employment opportunities in the USA and Australia. Additionally, Nclex Nepal is committed to supporting healthcare professionals in their pursuit of career growth in the UK, offering a specialized OET pathway for seamless relocation.

Established in 2019, Nclex Nepal has rapidly evolved into a dynamic organization dedicated to empowering doctors and nurses from Nepal to achieve their dreams of becoming international healthcare professionals. With a track record of assisting over 3000 individuals, we stand as a testament to the transformative impact we have had on the lives and careers of healthcare aspirants.

What sets us apart in our 5 years of operation is not just our youthfulness but the distinctive heights we have scaled in the field. At the core of our success lies a set of unwavering work ethics that drive our commitment to excellence. Our success stories are not just narratives; they are living testimonials of the quality of education, guidance, and support that every individual receives at Nclex Nepal.
