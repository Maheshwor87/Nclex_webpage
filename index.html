Frequent asked questions

Q.Who can attend the session?

A.This seminar is exclusively for NURSES, including those with BSC, BN, and PCL qualifications.

Q.Is entry to the seminar free?

A.Yes, entry to the seminar is absolutely free. We believe in providing valuable information to aspiring nurses without any financial barriers.

Q.Is Registration required?

A.Yes, registration is compulsory.

Q.How do I register for the seminar?

A.To sign up for the session, visit the Instagram profile page at “nclexnepal.official“ and click the link provided in the bio section.

Q.What are the key topics that will be covered in the seminar?

A.The seminar will cover crucial aspects such as:

  • NCLEX: National Council Licensure Examination
  • OET: Occupational English Test)
  • OBA: Outcome Based Assessment.
  • Costs: Breakdown of expenses associated with exams, certification, and relocation.
  • Opportunities for Nurses: Comprehensive discussion on career prospects and pathways in the UK, USA, and Australia.

Q.What are the key points that will be addressed during the seminar?

  • Visa Processes: Guidance on navigating visa procedures for each destination.
  • Credential Evaluation: Understanding the process of having your credentials recognized in these countries.
  • Job Placement Assistance: Information on resources and support available for securing nursing positions.
  • Networking Opportunities: Information on building professional connections within the nursing community.
  • Q&A Session: A dedicated time for attendees to ask specific questions and clarify doubts.

Q.Can I bring a friend?

A.Yes, you are welcome to bring a friend who is interested in exploring nursing opportunities abroad. However, please ensure they are nurses and also register to guarantee their seat.

Q.How long is the seminar expected to last?

A.The seminar is scheduled to last approximately 3 hours. This includes presentations, Q&A sessions, and networking opportunities.

Q.How can I contact the organizers for further information?

A.For any additional queries, you can reach out to our organizers at 01-4168241/103.
